# Kitchen Reset

## A Few Things Before You Go

> A tidy kitchen is a gift to the next person — including future you.

### 🍽️ Dishes

1. Grab a dry towel.
2. Dry and put away the clean dishes from the rack.
3. Dry and put away the clean cutlery too.
4. Have a look around for any stray dishes or cutlery — pop them in the sink.
5. Give the dirty dishes and cutlery a rinse.
6. Hang the towel back on the copper pipe.

---

### 🧽 Work Surfaces

1. Tuck perishables back in the fridge.
2. Return salt and pepper to the shelf.
3. Put everything else back where it lives.
4. Give the surfaces a good wipe.

---

### 🛋️ Couch

1. Fold the blankets.
2. Fluff and arrange the cushions.

---

### 🧹 Floor

- Give it a sweep.

---

### 🔥 Heater

- Switch it off or reset it.

---

## *Intention*

*Leave the room feeling calm, welcoming, and ready for whatever comes next.*
